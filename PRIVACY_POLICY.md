# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 12, 2026**

## Introduction

SwiftCraft ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SwiftCraft (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Name**: You may optionally provide your first name during onboarding to personalize your experience.
- **Apple ID**: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier and optionally your name. We never receive your email address.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: We track your progress within the App (completed challenges, XP earned, streaks, badges earned) to provide the gamified learning experience. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your private iCloud account if you enable iCloud sync.
- **Purchase History**: When you make in-app purchases, Apple's StoreKit provides us with information about which products you've purchased. We do not receive payment information.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or media
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics data (we use no third-party analytics)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:
- Provide and personalize the App's learning experience
- Track your progress, XP, streaks, and badges
- Restore your purchases across devices
- Sync your progress via iCloud (if enabled)
- Send optional streak reminder notifications (with your permission)

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
Most of your data is stored locally on your device using:
- **SwiftData**: Your learning progress, completed challenges, XP, and streaks
- **UserDefaults**: Your preferences (sound, haptics, notifications) and onboarding status
- **Keychain**: Your Apple ID identifier (if you sign in)

### iCloud Sync
If you enable iCloud Sync in Settings, your progress data is synced to your private iCloud account using CloudKit. This data is subject to Apple's privacy policy and is not accessible to us.

### In-App Purchases
Purchase information is managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2 and is tied to your Apple ID. We do not store or process payment information.

## Third-Party Services

SwiftCraft uses **no third-party services**, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks. The App is fully self-contained.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The only data that leaves your device is:
- iCloud sync data (stored in your private iCloud account, accessible only to you)
- StoreKit purchase verification (handled by Apple)

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device and/or iCloud account for as long as you use the App. You can delete all local data by uninstalling the App. iCloud data can be deleted through your iCloud settings.

## Children's Privacy

SwiftCraft is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (viewable within the App)
- Delete your data (uninstall the App or delete iCloud data)
- Opt out of notifications (in Settings or iOS Settings)
- Disable iCloud sync (in Settings)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page.
