# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 13, 2026**

## Introduction

SwiftCraft ("we," "our," or "us") is built to respect your privacy completely. This Privacy Policy explains how SwiftCraft handles your information — and, importantly, why **we do not collect any of it**.

## We Do Not Collect Your Data

SwiftCraft has **no servers of its own**. We never receive, store, or process your personal information. Everything the App needs stays in one of two places:

1. **On your device** (using Apple's on-device storage), or
2. **In your own private iCloud account** (which only you can access — not us), and only if you choose to enable iCloud Sync.

Because we operate no backend, there is no database somewhere holding your data. The App is fully self-contained.

## Information the App Uses (and Where It Lives)

### Information You Provide
- **Name**: You may optionally enter your first name during onboarding to personalize your experience. It is stored only on your device.
- **Sign in with Apple**: If you choose to sign in, the App receives a unique identifier (and optionally your name) directly from Apple, on your device. We never receive your email address, and this identifier never leaves your device except into your own private iCloud (if sync is on).

### Information Created as You Learn
- **Progress Data**: Your completed challenges, XP, streaks, and badges are stored locally on your device — and optionally in your private iCloud account if you enable iCloud Sync. We have no access to it.
- **Purchase Records**: When you make an in-app purchase, Apple's StoreKit tells the App which products you own so it can unlock them. This is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive payment information, and we store no purchase data on any server.

### Information We Never Touch
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or media
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics data (we use no third-party analytics)

## How Your Data Is Used (On Your Device)

The information above is used only by the App, on your device, to:
- Provide and personalize the learning experience
- Track your progress, XP, streaks, and badges
- Unlock and restore your purchases
- Sync your progress via your private iCloud (if you enable it)
- Send optional streak reminder notifications (with your permission)

None of this involves transmitting your data to us or to any third party.

## Where Your Data Is Stored

### On Your Device
Most of your data is stored locally using:
- **SwiftData**: Your learning progress, completed challenges, XP, and streaks
- **UserDefaults**: Your preferences (sound, haptics, notifications) and onboarding status
- **Keychain**: Your Sign in with Apple identifier (if you sign in)

### Your Private iCloud
If you enable iCloud Sync in Settings, your progress data syncs to **your own** private iCloud account via CloudKit. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is **not accessible to us**.

### In-App Purchases
Purchase information is managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2 and tied to your Apple ID. We do not store or process payment information.

## Third-Party Services

SwiftCraft uses **no third-party services**, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks. The App is fully self-contained.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information — because we never hold it in the first place. The only data that leaves your device is:
- iCloud sync data (stored in your private iCloud account, accessible only to you)
- StoreKit purchase verification (handled by Apple)

## Data Retention & Deletion

Your data lives on your device and/or in your iCloud account for as long as you use the App. You can delete all local data by uninstalling the App, and your iCloud data through your iCloud settings. Since we hold none of your data, there is nothing for us to delete on our side.

## Children's Privacy

SwiftCraft is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App collects no data at all, no personal information from any user — child or adult — ever reaches us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (viewable within the App)
- Delete your data (uninstall the App or delete iCloud data)
- Opt out of notifications (in Settings or iOS Settings)
- Disable iCloud sync (in Settings)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page.
